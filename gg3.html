Overview

Birla Pravaah, Gurgaon is a new residential project that offers premium 3 BHK and 3.5 BHK apartments. The project is located in Sector 71, one of the fast-growing areas of Gurgaon. It is designed to give modern families a perfect balance of comfort, style and convenience. The location is another big advantage. Birla Pravaah is well connected to NH-48, Sohna Road and Golf Course Extension Road. Schools, hospitals, shopping malls and offices are nearby, making it a very convenient address for modern living. Birla Pravaah is a great choice for people who want a premium home in the heart of Gurgaon.
